Melee DPS

Reaper

The Reaper is a relentless melee fighter who thrives in close-quarters combat. Armed with scythes and dark energy, Reapers carve through enemies with devastating combos and life-draining abilities.

Role Melee DPS
Difficulty Hard
Playstyle Aggressive

Origins of the Reaper

Long before the arenas were forged, the Reapers walked between worlds — harvesting the souls of the fallen and binding them to an ancient covenant. They were neither living nor dead, but something in between: sentinels of the threshold, guardians of the passage. When the corruption spread, the Reapers were the first to feel its pull — and the first to weaponize it.

The Dark Covenant

Every Reaper is bound by a blood pact — a ritual that strips away mortal fears in exchange for an insatiable hunger for combat. Their scythes are not mere weapons; they are conduits to the void, each strike siphoning life force from enemies and feeding the Reaper's dark power. In battle, they become an unstoppable storm of blades, growing stronger with every soul they claim.
