Assassin

The Assassin is a shadow-dwelling predator who strikes from the unseen. Masters of stealth and precision, Assassins eliminate high-value targets before vanishing back into darkness.

Role Stealth DPS
Difficulty Very Hard
Playstyle Ambush Predator

Born in Shadow

The Assassins of OKUBI are remnants of an ancient guild that operated in the spaces between the floating islands — the "Hollow Corridors" where light never reaches. They learned to become one with the darkness, moving without sound, striking without warning. Their training begins in childhood, in lightless chambers where survival depends on senses beyond sight.

The Silent Contract

Every Assassin carries a single mark carved into their blade — the name of their first kill. It serves as both a reminder and a promise: no target escapes, no contract goes unfulfilled. In the arenas, they are ghosts with daggers — appearing only for the killing blow before dissolving back into nothing.
