Invoker

The Invoker channels raw elemental power to devastate the battlefield. Masters of fire, ice, and lightning, Invokers control zones and unleash catastrophic spells from range.

Role Caster
Difficulty Hard
Playstyle Zone Control

Weavers of the Elements

Invokers are scholars who sought forbidden knowledge in the Arcane Spires — towers that pierce the boundary between the physical and elemental planes. Through decades of study and ritual scarring, they learned to channel raw elemental energy through their bodies. Fire, ice, and lightning bend to their will — but the power demands a price that few are willing to pay.

The Elemental Triad

An Invoker's mastery lies in their ability to shift between three elemental attunements in the heat of combat. Fire brings devastation, ice brings control, and lightning brings precision. The greatest Invokers weave all three into a single, continuous assault — an ever-shifting storm that adapts to any threat and overwhelms any defense.
