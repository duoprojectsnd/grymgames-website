Pathfinder

The Pathfinder is a precision-based ranged combatant who dominates from a distance. Masters of the bow and aerial mobility, Pathfinders rain death from above while staying out of reach.

Role Ranged DPS
Difficulty Medium
Playstyle Kite & Strike

Eyes Above the Canopy

Pathfinders were once the silent scouts of the Winged Legions — cartographers of the sky who mapped the invisible currents between floating islands. They learned to read the wind like a language, to strike from angles no ground-fighter could anticipate. When the corruption fractured the airways, the Pathfinders became the only ones who could navigate the chaos.

The Horizon's Edge

A Pathfinder's bow is more than a weapon — it is an extension of their sight. Arrows imbued with wind magic can curve around obstacles, pierce through barriers, and rain down from impossible heights. In the arenas, they are the unseen hunters: by the time you hear the arrow, it's already too late.
