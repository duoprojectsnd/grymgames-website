Guardian

The Guardian is an unyielding fortress on the battlefield. Masters of shield and armor, Guardians protect allies and control the flow of combat through crowd control and defensive mastery.

Role Tank
Difficulty Medium
Playstyle Protector

The Eternal Shield

The Guardians are the last line of defense — an ancient order sworn to protect the realms from annihilation. Clad in living armor that grows stronger with each battle, they stand where others fall. Their shields are not merely weapons; they are relics passed down through generations, each one carrying the memory of every blow it has ever absorbed.

Unbreakable Oath

To become a Guardian is to take an oath that transcends death itself. They channel the collective will of their fallen brothers into an impenetrable wall of steel and light. When a Guardian plants their feet and raises their shield, entire armies halt. Their presence alone changes the tide of war — not through destruction, but through the absolute refusal to yield.
