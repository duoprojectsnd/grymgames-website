Berserker

The Berserker is a fury-driven powerhouse who grows stronger the longer the fight goes on. Wielding massive axes and hammers, Berserkers overwhelm opponents with raw aggression and unstoppable momentum.

Role Bruiser
Difficulty Medium
Playstyle Sustained Brawler

Children of the Storm

Born in the scorched highlands where lightning never ceases, the Berserkers are forged through endless conflict. Their clans worship the fury of the storm itself — each warrior branded at birth with marks that burn brighter with every battle. To fight is to pray, and to rage is to ascend. They know no retreat, only the next swing.

The Fury Within

A Berserker's rage is not mere anger — it is a living force that courses through their veins like molten iron. The longer they fight, the more powerful they become, their bodies absorbing punishment that would fell lesser warriors. In the arenas of OKUBI, a wounded Berserker is the most dangerous opponent you'll ever face.
